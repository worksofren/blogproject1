Notes From My Flexbox Lessons:

The (fr) unit represents a fraction of available space in this container.

repeat() notation can be used to quickly layout many tracks for large grids.

grid-template-columns:1fr 1fr 1fr 1fr ;(spans 4 columns) could be written as grid-template-columns:repeat(4,1fr);
